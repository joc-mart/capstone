Process

Research

I compiled resources in a variety of media forms, interviewed and surveyed potential users, and interviewed two mental health professionals. For feedback on the first draft of the prototype, I surveyed participants who volunteered their emails in the first survey.

Tools

Surveys were conducted using Google Forms and and analyzed using Google Sheets. I created the prototype on Figma, the brand style guide using Adobe InDesign, and the illustrations on Procreate.

Questions

How to present resources in an accessible and visually appealing way?

What resources would be helpful? What tools actually work?

How will the prototype keep the attention of the user?

What information beyond resources should be included?
