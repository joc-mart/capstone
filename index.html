What is it?

Dysfunction Junction is a collection of resources to understand and alleviate executive dysfunction

Problem Statement

Executive dysfunction is a set of symptoms including difficulty with following multiple- step directions, meeting goals, prioritizing tasks, and impulse-control. Some people have executive dysfunction as part of ADHD, as a result of brain damage, or degenerative brain diseases. Personally, I have ADHD and struggle with executive dysfunction and the day to day experience is taxing. This project aims to compile resources and create community for those who struggle with any or all symptoms of executive dysfunction.
